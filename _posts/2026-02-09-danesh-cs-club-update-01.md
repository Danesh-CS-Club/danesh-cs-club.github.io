# Danesh Computer Science Club / Update 01

In this first update, we're sharing the latest from the club. Here's what's happened so far.

- Applied for registry on Code Club (https://codeclub.org/), an online platform run by the Raspberry Pi Foundation. With over 2 million young people involved worldwide (no clubs registered in an 80km radius of Tehran, Isfahan, or Iran).

- Started the weekly newsletter. Members post critical updates about advancements in technology each week.

- New club member: Seyyed Parham Heydari.

- Launched a website to publish lectures and notes.

- Registered the official YouTube channel.

- First group project and the introduction to Git have been published. BFS and DFS, Introduction to RTOS, and introduction to DevOps are still left.
